Lasagna Recipe

Description

Lasagna, the ultimate comfort food! This classic recipe is a crowd-pleaser, with layers of tender pasta, rich meat sauce, and a blend of creamy cheeses. It’s perfect for family dinners, special occasions, or just a cozy night in.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 1 can (28 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a large skillet, cook the ground beef over medium-high heat until browned, breaking it up into small pieces as it cooks.
  4. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic to the skillet and cook until the onion is translucent.
  5. Stir in the crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper. Bring the sauce to a simmer and let it cook for 10-15 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  6. Let the lasagna rest for 10-15 minutes before slicing and serving. Garnish with chopped parsley, if desired.
