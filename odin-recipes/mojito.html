Mojito Recipe

Description

Mojito, the ultimate comfort food! This classic recipe is a crowd-pleaser, with layers of citron, menthe, and a blend of rum. It’s perfect for family dinners, special occasions, or just a cozy night in.

Ingredients

  • 14 fresh mint leaves
  • 2 fl oz light gold rum (1-3 year old molasses column
  • 1/3 fl oz sugar syrup ‘rich’ (2 sugar to 1 water, 65.0°Brix)
  • 1/2 fl oz Thomas Henry Soda Water
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil

Steps

  1. Muddle the lime juice, sugar, and mint leaves in a small jug, crushing the mint as you go.
  2. Pour the mixture into a tall glass filled with ice.
  3. Add the rum and stir well.
  4. Top up with soda water and stir gently.
  5. Garnish with a sprig of fresh mint and serve.
